## What This Solution Does

Want to know which areas in your store have the most foot traffic? This solution turns reCamera footage into intuitive traffic heatmaps while automatically blurring people to protect privacy.

## Key Benefits

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Visualize Traffic Patterns | No need to watch monitors - see at a glance where people gather |
| Automatic Privacy Protection | Faces are blurred on the camera itself before any data leaves |
| Data Stays Local | All data stored on your computer, nothing uploaded to cloud |
| Historical Analysis | Compare traffic patterns across days and weeks |

## Use Cases

| Scenario | How to Use |
|----------|------------|
| Retail Stores | See which shelves attract most customers, optimize product placement |
| Trade Shows | Identify the most popular booths, improve future layouts |
| Office Spaces | Analyze desk and meeting room usage, optimize space planning |
| Restaurants & Cafes | Find the most popular seating areas, adjust furniture layout |

## Requirements

**Hardware**
- One reCamera
- A computer with Docker installed (Windows/Mac/Linux)

**Network**
- reCamera and computer must be on the same WiFi network

**Privacy**
- People are blurred on the reCamera locally - only coordinates are transmitted
- All data stays on your computer, nothing is uploaded to any server
